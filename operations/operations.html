Our Operations



Gokak Mill Old Hydro Power Plant


This old power house situated near mill generates 3.5 MW for about 8 months .It utilizes flows from the Ghataprabha river with a gross head of 56 metres at Gokak Falls . It contains 5 units . The first three (1,2,3) each of 500 KW capacity were commissioned in 1914 followed by unit 4 having capacity of 1000KW . Later from 1990 to 1992 all old turbines were replaced by new turbines of higher effeciency, supplied by M/s Boving Fouress Ltd. Bangalore .



D.J.Madan Power Plant


Dhupdal Hydropower station (D.J.Madan Powerhouse) is situated near Dhupdal dam which is about 8km from Gokak Falls. This powerhouse is canal based( Ghatprabha left bank canal) & consists of 2 units of 1.4 MW each and generates 2.8 MW . This Hydropower station was commissioned during the year 1996 .



HFO Power Plant


The HFO (Heavy Fuel Oil) power plant has an installed capacity of 6300KW. This power plant was commssioned in the year 2004 & the power is utilized for capacitive purpose .



Monsoon Spillway Project


The availability of flood water from Ghataprabha river during monsoon for 100 days , it is proposed to install 4.5 MW capacity next to power house-II at Gokak Falls. This was commsissioned during the month of June 2009 .
